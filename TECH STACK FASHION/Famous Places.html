Mughal Sarai

The name Mughal Sarai itself invokes history and tourists visit this structure in droves even today to imagine what it must have been like to travel during the Mughal era. The structure was previously used as an inn or Sarai and today the building houses many government offices of Surat Municipality. The stunning art work and intricate engravings on the arches still proudly proclaim the grandeur of Mughal heritage. There are a number of cafes and restaurants nearby where one can enjoy authentic Gujarati cuisine.
Timings: Open 24 hours
Location: At the heart of the city
Entry Fee: N/A

The Tomb of Khudawand Khan

Located near Chakla Bazaar, the tomb happens to be the resting place of one of the most revered governors of Surat, Khudawand Khan. It is said that he did much for the prosperity of the city and to promote it as a commercial hub, for which it is famous till today. The tomb has wonderful Islamic engravings and the architecture is stunning. The Chakla Bazaar nearby is itself one of the most popular places to visit in Surat for shopping where one can pick out traditional Bandhni dupattas, accessories like cloth bags and jootis and glass bangles.
Timings: 6 am to 6 pm
Location: Chakla Bazaar Road
Entry Fee: N/A

Rang Upvan

The biggest outdoor amphitheater in Surat, Rang Upvan frequently plays host to notable Gujarati plays and many respected theater personalities have performed here. Apart from plays, musical concerts and even yoga sessions are held here. The grounds are almost always booked and groups and performers have to book the venue months in advance to get a slot here. Anyone who looks forward to exploring the cultural scene of Surat must come here for some of the performances.

Amaazia Waterpark

Counted among the biggest water parks in Asia, Amaazia Waterpark is also considered as the best one in its city Surat. This great combination of amusement park and distinct buying options makes up for a great weekend escape for your entire family.

Suvali Beach

Snuggled away from the chaos of the city the Suvali beach is one of the most serene places to see in Surat. The beach offers charcoal black sand basked with the clear waters, where you can dip down your legs and relax. The vibes of the beach are quite comforting and the sunsets make it even more peaceful.
