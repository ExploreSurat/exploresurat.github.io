About

Surat is a city located in the Indian state of Gujarat. It is the eighth largest city in India and is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and fast-growing economy. Surat is often referred to as the "Silk City" due to its thriving textile industry and is one of the largest producers of silk in India. The city is also famous for its diamond cutting and polishing industry, which is one of the largest in the world.
